📚 Best Study Techniques for Productivity

Studying efficiently is not about spending more hours but using smarter techniques.

**Top Study Techniques:**

  • ⏳ **Pomodoro Technique** - Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.
  • 📖 **Feynman Technique** - Simplify complex concepts by teaching them to yourself.
  • 🖋️ **Active Recall** - Self-test using flashcards instead of passive reading.
  • 📌 **Spaced Repetition** - Review information at strategic intervals for better retention.

These methods enhance memory, concentration, and overall learning efficiency.
